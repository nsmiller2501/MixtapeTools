# /grill-me

An adversarial interview skill. Invoke it when you want Claude to challenge your plan or design systematically rather than agreeing with you. Claude works through the full decision tree one branch at a time, asking pointed questions and providing its own recommended answer for each — so you either confirm the direction or push back with a reason. Stops when every decision is resolved.
